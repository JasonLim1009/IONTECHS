Our Story

14 Years of Innovation in Healthy building Materials

For 14 Years, we have been at the forefront of Innovation, starting with our own factory producing negative ION Flooring and ION PAINT. Over the years, we have expanded across Malaysia and into international markets, continually developing premium building materials that promote healthier living. Our commitment remains the same - to create spaces that are not only beautiful but also beneficial to well-being.

ION WOOD

Multi-Layer Engineered Wood Flooring is crafted from tropical forest wood, combinig simplicity and elegance, Its core consists of 7 to 9 Layers of plywood, while the surface features Kembang Semangkuk (KSK), Merbau, Hevea, Walnut, White Oak or custom veneers. Layers are glued and heat-pressed into durable 9-15mm T&G floorboards, showcasing natural wood beauty.
