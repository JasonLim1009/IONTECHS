Best Seller

ION SPC

Stone Plastic Composite Flooring is crafted for durability and stability, combinig natural aesthetics with advanced technology. Its core consists of a rigid ION SPC Layer that resists expansion and contraction, while the surface features high-quality wood or stone textures. The layers are fused using heat and pressure, forming a strong, waterproof and dimensionally stable flooring solution with a total thickness of 6.5mm, designed for both residential And commercial spaces.
